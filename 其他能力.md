<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt"><font face="宋体">如：你拥有</font>“若你对目标造成伤害，则对该目标造成相当于伤害数的燃烧点数。”你对目标造成3点伤害，同时额外造成3点寒冰伤害，则对目标额外造成6点燃烧点数。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt"><font face="宋体">若附加的额外伤害本身会带来额外的效果，则该额外的效果只计算本能力对应的额外伤害效果，原本的伤害与其他的额外伤害与此无关。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><b><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; COLOR: rgb(43,43,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt"><font face="宋体">替代检定</font></span></b><b><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; COLOR: rgb(43,43,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></b></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt"><font face="宋体">在某种情况下，当角色进行检定时，其原本的检定中骰子构成部分会被其他的内容代替。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt"><font face="宋体">如：白刃攻击检定使用的是力量</font>+白刃+武器伤害，当其获得“你可以使用敏捷代替力量进行白刃攻击检定”时，其肉搏攻击检定变为敏捷+白刃+武器伤害。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt"><font face="宋体">除非有特殊说明，否则代替的内容无法代替其原本内容的衍生属性，以及衍生内容，并且也无法二次替代。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt"><font face="宋体">如：使用敏捷代替力量检定，无法使用敏捷来计算伤害上限。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt"><font face="宋体">如：如果风度代替攻击检定，无法使用风度来进行代替智力进行施法中的攻击检定。</font></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt">如：使用敏捷代替力量进行攻击检定，使用风度代替敏捷进行攻击检定，若该攻击检定原本是力量，则无法使用风度代替力量进行攻击检定。</span></p>
