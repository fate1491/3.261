<p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt"><font face="宋体">所有精灵都拥有独特的</font><font face="宋体">“灵装”与“天使”，它们分别有以下特性：</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
