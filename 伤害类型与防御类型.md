<p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt">===========================================================================================================</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><b><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; COLOR: rgb(43,43,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">伤害类型：</font></span></b><b><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; COLOR: rgb(43,43,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></b></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">本游戏中所有的伤害皆有伤害类型。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">若一个伤害带有复数伤害类型，比如一个伤害既包括火焰伤害和神圣伤害，则必须有对应的全部伤害减免才可以生效，若想要免疫，则也必须拥有对应全部的免疫能力才可以生效。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">如造成了</font>5点火焰且神圣伤害，目标拥有3点神圣抗力和2点火焰抗力，则可以抵消2点伤害。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">当你没有受到一个伤害之后，则该伤害附带的效果则不会生效。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt">===========================================================================================================</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><b><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; COLOR: rgb(43,43,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">物理伤害</font></span></b><b><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; COLOR: rgb(43,43,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></b></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">由实际的物体打击、切割、造成形变，从而造成的伤害称为物理伤害。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">这包括了各种物质的武器或临时武器，如刀、棍棒、牙齿、半截酒瓶等等。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">有些对象免疫普通物理伤害，这有可能是因为它们不具备物质的实体（如灵魂），或者物理上的形态改变对它没有意义（如流水、雾）。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">也有些对象对物理伤害有一定的抗性，如物理伤害减免（</font>DR），这意味着它异常坚固，或者被某种奇妙的超自然力量保护着。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">物理伤害也会因为造成的方式分为伤害类型，如</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; COLOR: rgb(255,0,0); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">挥砍、穿刺、钝击以</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">突破一些特殊的伤害减免。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">同时，我们将拥有</font>“弹药”特性以及“无限弹药”但是无法造成能量伤害的枪械，弩，弓箭等造成的伤害划分为实弹武器伤害，这些伤害都是穿刺伤害。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; COLOR: rgb(255,0,0); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">有些魔法或者特殊能力也会造成无类型物理伤害，如果这种伤害没有确定物理伤害类型，那么它造成的默认是物理伤害，不再进行区分，在面对对应的物理伤害的防御类型上，其不作为任意类型判定。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; COLOR: rgb(255,0,0); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">（如：免疫物理钝击伤害，无法免疫这些能力造成的普通物理伤害。如：当你造成穿刺伤害时，你可以额外对目标造成</font>5点伤害，则这些能力造成的普通物理伤害也无法触发本效果）</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">物理伤害作用于物体与生物时，先被硬度抵消，每</font>1点硬度抵消1点伤害，然后造成全部伤害。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt">===========================================================================================================</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><b><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; COLOR: rgb(43,43,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">能量伤害</font></span></b><b><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; COLOR: rgb(43,43,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></b></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">由非物质的能量造成的伤害。能量伤害不受物理伤害减免（</font>DR）的影响，但会被“能量抗力”减免。也有一些效果使对象完全免疫某种能量伤害。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">能量伤害一半包括以下几类：</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; COLOR: rgb(255,0,0); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">纯能量伤害：</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">这是一类只能以</font>“能量”描述的能量伤害，效果无法用物理作用解释。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">纯能量伤害作用于物体时，先被硬度抵消，每</font>1点硬度抵消1点伤害，然后造成余量1/2的伤害。作用于生物时，先被硬度抵消，每1点硬度抵消1点伤害，然后造成余量全部伤害。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; COLOR: rgb(255,0,0); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">火焰伤害（又名灼烧伤害，灼热伤害）：</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">高温造成的伤害，比如火焰、滚烫的蒸汽等。包括因高温焚化而使物质损失、或者因高温导至身体部分细胞死亡等。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">火焰伤害作用于物体时，先被硬度抵消，每</font>1点硬度抵消1点伤害，然后造成余量1/2的伤害。作用于生物时，先被硬度抵消，每1点硬度抵消1点伤害，然后造成余量全部伤害。（并且可以点燃易燃易爆物，如纸屑，干草，油桶，炸药等，是否为易燃易爆物由ST判断）</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; COLOR: rgb(255,0,0); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">寒冰伤害（又名冻寒伤害）：</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">低温造成的伤害，包括因低温使生物体内液体冻结、细胞活性下降，或者只是低温造成的破裂等物理效应。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">寒冰伤害作用于物体时，先被硬度抵消，每</font>1点硬度抵消1点伤害，然后造成余量1/2的伤害。作用于生物时，先被硬度抵消，每1点硬度抵消1点伤害，然后造成余量全部伤害。（并且可以冻结液体，如水等，具体的冻结效果和强度由ST判断）</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">毒素伤害就是由于生物受到了毒素的影响而受到的伤害，这种毒素一般都是通过某种方式，比如造成伤害或者吸入等，来让目标中毒，中毒后的目标受到毒素效果影响。</font></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt">但是需要注意的是，免疫毒素影响并不会免疫毒素伤害，除非该毒素伤害本身是毒素效果。</span><o:p></o:p></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">毒素伤害作用于物体时，无法造成伤害。作用于生物时，无视硬度，造成全部伤害，并且无视目标的伤害减免能力，同时无视目标由物品带来的伤害降低能力（伤害吸收，伤害忽略，伤害抵消，伤害减免）以及物品带来的伤害降低效果（恶性-严重-冲击）。</font></span><o:p></o:p></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt">===========================================================================================================</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><b><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; COLOR: rgb(43,43,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">伤害易伤</font></span></b><b><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; COLOR: rgb(43,43,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></b></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">以上的所有伤害皆有</font>“易伤”，若没有特殊说明，则当目标拥有对应伤害的“易伤”时，则目标受到的该伤害的最终伤害翻倍。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">若目标拥有对应伤害的</font>“易伤X”时，则目标受到的对应伤害的最终伤害增加X点，伤害级别与造成伤害中的较低部分相同。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">当目标受到混合伤害时，只要目标对应伤害的</font>“易伤”包括该混合伤害中的任一一种时，“易伤”效果即会生效。</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt">若没有特殊说明，则默认易伤无法叠加。</span><o:p></o:p></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">（如：目标拥有火焰易伤，当目标受到最终伤害为</font>2点火焰严重伤害，2点火焰冲击时，再额外受到2点火焰严重伤害，2点火焰冲击伤害。）</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">（如：目标拥有火焰易伤</font>2，当目标受到最终伤害为1点火焰冲击伤害，1点火焰严重伤害，则目标再额外受到2点火焰冲击伤害。）</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt">===========================================================================================================</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><b><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; COLOR: rgb(43,43,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">坠落伤害</font></span></b><b><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; COLOR: rgb(43,43,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></b></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">从高处掉下来，或者由于外力而撞击障碍物时，会受到坠落伤害。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">坠落伤害起始计算距离为</font>3米，在此之上每超过2米，就会受到1点物理钝击严重伤害，向上取整。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">角色可以进行反射检定来豁免伤害，每</font>1点成功可以抵消2米的距离。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">坠落伤害至多为10</font>0点物理钝击严重伤害，本伤害无视载具或者物品的硬度和阈值。</span><o:p></o:p></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">若角色因此受到了坠落伤害，则角色倒地。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">主动进行跳跃时，坠落从超过跳跃距离之后开始计算。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">若坠落物下方有较为柔软的地形，每米高度能抵消十倍距离，因此一块</font>3米厚的充气垫能抵消30米坠落距离。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">坠落目标在坠落的第一轮会坠落</font>180米，第二轮变为360米，第三轮变为720米，第四轮开始每轮1000米到达坠落极限，1000米/轮的坠落速度也是地球上自由落体坠落伤害的上限，改变重力环境可以改变这一速度上限（由ST判断）。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">坠落距离在角色行动结束时发生，当角色在行动未结束时受到坠落效果（例如一脚踏空）时，其将处于浮空状态。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">处于坠落中的目标若没有飞行能力或其他特殊的效果，通常始终处于浮空状态，直到落地。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">若目标具有飞行能力，其不会因移动（如一脚踏空）而坠落，但依旧可能因其他效果而坠落，但即便如此，只要具有飞行能力，坠落时不会处于浮空状态，但依旧会正常计算坠落距离。且其在回合开始时处于向正下方移动的过程，视他的机动性，他可能需要一些额外的操作来调整自己的方向。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">若坠落中的目标获得过飞行能力，并成功改变过自己向正下方移动的过程，坠落距离和速度就会重新开始计算，而非提升之前的坠落速度（例如一个坠落中的角色用梯云纵提气以完美速度向上移动了哪怕</font>1米，坠落距离都会被重置）</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">有飞行能力的目标可以在自己的回合中不进行任何飞行移动来宣布停用飞行能力以在自身行动结束时启动计算坠落距离（并且因为他有飞行能力并不会处于浮空）。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">传送效果可以重置坠落距离的计算。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt">===========================================================================================================</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><b><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; COLOR: rgb(43,43,255); FONT-STYLE: normal; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">坠落物伤害</font></span></b><b><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; COLOR: rgb(43,43,255); FONT-STYLE: normal; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></b></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">从高处自然掉下来的坠落目标对被击中的另一个目标构成的伤害。不包括使用任何效果（包括从高处射击，或从空中向下移动等等）。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">初始计算距离为</font>3米，每高出1米即有1DP，命中目标后，物体的重量加权（即物品的重量/5）会作为附加成功对目标造成物理严重伤害，目标可以进行反射豁免来减少受到的伤害，每个成功数豁免1点伤害。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">坠落物伤害至多为</font>20点。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">坠物伤害是一种物理伤害，伤害类型取决于坠落物本身。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">如：物品的重量加权为</font>5，其从100米高度下落，其DP为100DP+5附加成功，最终结果为35点伤害，由于伤害上限为20，则目标进行反射豁免，成功数为10点，此时造成的伤害为10点。</span><o:p></o:p></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">若坠落物的体型大于目标</font>2级（如目标小型生物，坠物物为大型物体）且在坠落后并未被摧毁，则其有可能将目标压在下面，目标相当于被擒抱，逃脱擒抱的DC为坠落物的体积值。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">若坠落物的体型未大于目标</font>2级也未小于目标2级，则坠落物会落入目标的相邻格内.</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">若坠落物的体型小于目标</font>2级（如目标大型生物，坠落物为小型物体），则坠落物可以落入与目标的同一格。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">注：某些坠落物无论多高也不会造成任何伤害，如水，海绵球等。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">注：由于力的作用于反作用效果，一个物品只有受到坠落伤害才可以如常造成坠落物伤害。如果其以任何效果免疫了坠落伤害（包括灵体化等），它也就无法造成坠落物伤害。</font></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt">若有目标在高处对处于低处的目标进行坠落物攻击，则需要进行远程投掷攻击检定，根据检定结果判定是否砸中。若砸中，则将坠落物伤害作为额外伤害进行结算。</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt">这意味着若目标处于极限投掷射程之外，则几乎无法砸中。</span><o:p></o:p></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt">===========================================================================================================</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><b><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; COLOR: rgb(43,43,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">混合伤害</font></span></b><b><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; COLOR: rgb(43,43,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></b></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">某种情况下，伤害类型可能会混合。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">如：该伤害既为火焰亵渎伤害。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">此时，针对该伤害，你必须拥有全部对应其伤害类型的防御能力（吸收，抵消，减免，忽略，反弹等）才可以对其全部生效。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">如：你受到</font>5点火焰混合亵渎伤害，此时你有2点火焰抗力和1点亵渎抗力，则你最终受到4点伤害。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">如：你受到</font>5点火焰混合亵渎伤害，此时你有3点全能量抗力，则你最终受到2点伤害。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">如：你受到</font>5点火焰混合物理伤害，此时你免疫火焰伤害，并且拥有3点物理伤害减免，则你最终受到2点伤害。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">混合伤害有可能会出现火焰伤害与寒冰伤害混合（如霜火箭），这是正常情况，如常结算伤害和特效即可。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">混合伤害中的伤害特性如果不一样，如光能伤害混合火焰伤害，光能伤害无视硬度，而火焰伤害受到硬度影响，这种情况下，以伤害方取优判定，不冲突的效果可以叠加生效。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt">===========================================================================================================</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><b><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; COLOR: rgb(43,43,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">多种伤害同时出现</font></span></b></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><b><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; COLOR: rgb(43,43,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"></span></b><font face="宋体" size="3">某种情况下，同一次攻击可能会包括多次攻击。</font></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><font face="宋体" size="3">如：武器可以造成挥砍或者穿刺伤害，这种情况下，你选择其中一个伤害类型生效。</font></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><font face="宋体" size="3">如：武器可以造成挥砍伤害，发动一个技能，使本次攻击造成雷电伤害，这种情况下，技能带来的伤害类型覆盖原本武器造成的伤害。</font></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><font face="宋体" size="3">如：武器可以造成挥砍伤害，发动一次技能，使武器改为造成雷电伤害，这种情况下，武器造成的伤害为改之后的伤害。</font></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><font face="宋体" size="3">如：武器可以造成挥砍伤害，发动一次技能，使武器改为造成雷电伤害，然后又发动一个技能，本次攻击造成火焰伤害，这种情况下，技能带来的伤害类型覆盖原本武器造成的伤害。</font></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><font face="宋体" size="3">如：武器可以造成挥砍伤害，发动A技能，使本次攻击造成雷电伤害，发动B技能，使本次攻击造成火焰伤害，这种情况下，由发动者选择伤害类型。</font></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><font face="宋体" size="3">如：武器可以造成挥砍伤害，发动A技能，使武器改为造成火焰伤害，发动B技能，使武器改为造成雷电伤害，这种情况下，由发动者选择伤害类型。</font></p><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt">===========================================================================================================</span>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><b><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; COLOR: rgb(43,43,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">防御类型</font></span></b><b><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; COLOR: rgb(43,43,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></b></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">本游戏的所有伤害降低的能力只包括以下几种关键字说明：</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">豁免，吸收，抵消，转化，减免，忽略。不存在没有关键字说明的伤害降低。所有在资源中出现的伤害降低类能力都有其关键字。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">需要注意的是，任何防御能力都有可能被攻击方击破。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">例：无视目标</font>X点能量抗力。此时针对攻击方的击破能力，防御方若有多个对应的防御能力，由攻击方依次击破，击破顺序从防御方的又高到低开始击破（若相同，则由攻击方决定），必须将一个防御能力全部击破为0，若有剩余才可以击破下一个同类型的防御能力。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">伤害吸收是某种使伤害无效化的能力。伤害吸收</font>X代表，在受到的每一次伤害之中，有X点被吸收，也就是无效化。伤害吸收有三种类型：</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">物理伤害减免某种使物理伤害无效化的能力。伤害减免</font>X代表，在受到的每一次物理伤害中，有X点被减免，也就是无效化。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">物理伤害减免只对物理伤害生效。大部分伤害减免都有一个弱点，对于特定的某种来源无效，这写作</font>DRX/某来源。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; COLOR: rgb(255,0,0); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt">DR/-：</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">这种伤害减免可以对所有物理伤害全部生效，这意味着该生物或者物体具备非常强大的物理抵抗能力，并且该能力没有任何弱点。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; COLOR: rgb(255,0,0); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt">DR/魔法：</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">具有</font>“【魔法】”特性的武器或者伤害可以穿透这种伤害减免。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; COLOR: rgb(255,0,0); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt">DR/神兵：</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">具有</font>“【神兵】”特性的武器或者伤害可以穿透这种伤害减免。有一些生物将强大的意志力量与物质性存在融合，因而坚固异常。只有同样具有强大意志力量的武器才能伤害它。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; COLOR: rgb(255,0,0); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt">DR/伤害类型：</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">如</font>DR/穿刺，DR/钝击，DR/挥砍。具备该伤害类型的武器或者伤害可以穿透这种伤害减免。这分别意味着有些生物体的表面结构坚固,可以抵挡穿透力不足的攻击，只有集力量于一点的刺击型武器才能刺入它的体内。而某些生物因内外结构坚实致密而可以抵挡刃器造成的划伤，只有钝击武器才能以沉重而浑厚的力量击碎它。还有一些生物体柔韧异常，大力的砸击或刺出小孔对它的生理功能无碍，只有宽阔的刃器对它造成又长又深的切割创口才能破坏它。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt">1）若是攻击，则防御附加成功扣除对方攻击的成功骰。若命中，判断防御方是否免疫该效果，攻击者无法在生效前得知防御方是否免疫。若不免疫，然后由攻击方算出本次攻击方的伤害结果，如命中造成的额外伤害等，伤害上限此时开始生效，命中后造成的额外伤害不计入伤害上限。</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt">1）若是豁免，且是固定伤害，则由防御方进行豁免对抗。若成功造成效果，判断防御方是否免疫该效果，攻击者无法在生效前得知防御方是否免疫。若不免疫，然后由攻击方算出本次攻击方的伤害结果，如命中造成的额外伤害等，命中后造成的额外伤害不计入伤害上限。</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt">1）<span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt">若是豁免，且是通过攻击/施法等方式造成浮动伤害，则进行对应检定，伤害上限此时开始生效，得到最终结构活，由防御方进行豁免对抗。若成功造成效果，判断防御方是否免疫该效果，攻击者无法在生效前得知防御方是否免疫。若不免疫，然后由攻击方算出本次攻击方的伤害结果，如命中造成的额外伤害等，命中后造成的额外伤害不计入伤害上限。</span></span><o:p></o:p></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt">2）攻击方计算伤害转化。（如【能量武器】等）</span><o:p></o:p></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt">3）记录攻击方的击破能力。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt">4）伤害免疫生效。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt">5）伤害忽略生效。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt">6）硬度与伤害抵消生效。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt">7）能量抗力或伤害减免生效。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt">8）伤害吸收生效。</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt">9）阈值生效。</span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt">10）防御方计算伤害转化（如【防能量武器】等），防御方按照盾牌-盔甲-角色本身的先后顺序生效。</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt">11）确定最终伤害，然后触发易伤，同时根据最终伤害情况结算的能力或者触发相关能力（如暴击）</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt">特殊：若在某一个阶段中有多个类似能力可以生效，则由目标自由决定顺序。</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt"><font face="宋体">特殊：若一个角色免疫冲击伤害，但是其伤害计算结果后伤害为严重伤害，之后由于角色的转化能力，将该伤害转化为了冲击伤害，则由于顺序已经过了免疫判断阶段，所以该冲击伤害如常有效。</font></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="LAYOUT-GRID-MODE: char; TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-layout-grid-align: none"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 0.0000pt">注意：因为暴击，易伤等类似能力都是在确定最终伤害后确定，所以其确定的方式都以当前的最终伤害为基准，如暴击翻倍和易伤和暴击1.5倍同时触发，最终伤害为10，则分别触发额外造成10（翻倍），额外造成10（易伤），额外造成5（1.5倍），目标获得的伤害为35.减伤同理。</span><o:p></o:p></p>
