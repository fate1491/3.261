<p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><b><span style="FONT-SIZE: 18pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt"><font face="宋体">货币介绍与获取：</font></span></b><b><span style="FONT-SIZE: 18pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; COLOR: rgb(0,0,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt"><o:p></o:p></span></b></p><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt">
<p class="MsoNormal" style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt 7.5pt 0pt 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; BACKGROUND: rgb(255,255,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-shading: rgb(255,255,255)"><font face="宋体">===========================================================================================================</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt 7.5pt 0pt 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; BACKGROUND: rgb(255,255,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-shading: rgb(255,255,255)"><font face="宋体">在无限恐怖</font><font face="宋体">TRPG中，有三种主要货币：</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt 7.5pt 0pt 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; BACKGROUND: rgb(255,255,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-shading: rgb(255,255,255)"><font face="宋体">支线：支线分为</font><font face="宋体">5种，分别为DCBAS。多数情况下，一个标价为C的能力几乎一定会比一个标价为D的能力要强大，而一个标价为B的能力也会相应比C能力更强。拥有一整套S级能力并且用他们打出一套华丽的连锁是每一个玩家的梦想。支线可以自由地拆分或组装。3个D可以拼成一个C，然后3个C可以拼成一个B，以此类推。同样的，反向的拆解也是可以进行的，一个C可以被拆开来购买3个不同的D支线资源。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt 7.5pt 0pt 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; BACKGROUND: rgb(255,255,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-shading: rgb(255,255,255)"><font face="宋体">奖励点数：又被称作分数，是无限流</font><font face="宋体">TRPG里最为常见的货币。一般情况下，你所能购买的资源都需要支付一定的支线和奖励点数。比如一杆高能粒子脉冲步枪的标价为D+500，这就意味着你需要支付一个D支线和500奖励点数来换取这一昂贵的未来科技武器。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt 7.5pt 0pt 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; BACKGROUND: rgb(255,255,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-shading: rgb(255,255,255)"><font face="宋体">经验：又称</font><font face="宋体">XP。XP对应表现在冒险世界中通过历练提升自己的实力。显而易见地，一个人获得的XP不可能转让给另外一个人。XP也不可能用来从无到有地生成某项能力，而是必须要在有一定基础的情况下进行“钻研”或“训练”来提升。更多的内容可以在“经验值系统”与“自创能力规则”这两页进行查询。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt 7.5pt 0pt 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; BACKGROUND: rgb(255,255,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-shading: rgb(255,255,255)"><font face="宋体">轮回之境不仅可以让参加者们在九死一生的冒险后好好休息，也提供了让他们变强的方法。在无限流</font><font face="宋体">TRPG中，你可以兑换几乎无限种类的不同能力。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt 7.5pt 0pt 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; BACKGROUND: rgb(255,255,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-shading: rgb(255,255,255)"><font face="宋体">消耗支线与奖励点数除非特殊情况，则必须在轮回世界才可以，兑换资源是一个自由动作。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt 7.5pt 0pt 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; BACKGROUND: rgb(255,255,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-shading: rgb(255,255,255)"><font face="宋体">消耗经验则可以在轮回世界和冒险世界中都可以直接消耗，若选择在冒险世界中，则没有特殊情况（如：光战，人皇）则需要消耗原本</font><font face="宋体">*2的经验，消耗经验习得新的能力无需动作。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt 7.5pt 0pt 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan"><b><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; BACKGROUND: rgb(255,255,255); FONT-WEIGHT: bold; COLOR: rgb(0,0,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-shading: rgb(255,255,255)"><font face="宋体">支线与分数奖励系统</font></span></b><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt 7.5pt 0pt 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; BACKGROUND: rgb(255,255,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-shading: rgb(255,255,255)"><font face="宋体">在无限流</font><font face="宋体">TRPG游戏规则之中，当角色击败了怪物或者完成了一项具有难度（一般是指有一定的生命危险）的挑战之后，我们将发放给玩家一些奖励，这些奖励是以支线和分数的形式出现的。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt 7.5pt 0pt 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; BACKGROUND: rgb(255,255,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-shading: rgb(255,255,255)"><font face="宋体">支线和分数可以用于购买轮回之境的各种强化，包括血统，改造，瞳术，称号，流派，魔导书等等。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt 7.5pt 0pt 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; BACKGROUND: rgb(255,255,255); COLOR: rgb(255,0,0); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-shading: rgb(255,255,255)"><font face="宋体">支线与分数的获得：</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt 7.5pt 0pt 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; BACKGROUND: rgb(255,255,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-shading: rgb(255,255,255)"><font face="宋体">1. 击败眼前的怪物，根据怪物可以对角色造成的威胁程度来定义该怪物的奖励。奖励可以是浮动的，因为同一只怪物在面对不同时期甚至不同角色的时候会造成的威胁不同。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt 7.5pt 0pt 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-char-indent-count: 0.0000; mso-para-margin-left: 0.0000gd"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; BACKGROUND: rgb(255,255,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-shading: rgb(255,255,255)"><font face="宋体">2. 解决眼前的难题，根据难题的困难程度以及必要程度和对角色的威胁定义该奖励的多少。如在哈利波特中摧毁死亡圣器等。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt 7.5pt 0pt 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-char-indent-count: 0.0000; mso-para-margin-left: 0.0000gd"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; BACKGROUND: rgb(255,255,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-shading: rgb(255,255,255)"><font face="宋体">3. 安全度过一个冒险世界，完成主线任务返回轮回之境，这是一个固定奖励。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; BACKGROUND: rgb(255,255,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-shading: rgb(255,255,255)"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt 7.5pt 0pt 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; mso-pagination: widow-orphan; mso-char-indent-count: 0.0000; mso-para-margin-left: 0.0000gd"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; BACKGROUND: rgb(255,255,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-shading: rgb(255,255,255)"><font face="宋体">4.  完成主神发放的支线任务，这可以是任何任务，取决于ST的安排。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; BACKGROUND: rgb(255,255,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-shading: rgb(255,255,255)"><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt 7.5pt 0pt 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan"><b><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; BACKGROUND: rgb(255,255,255); FONT-WEIGHT: bold; COLOR: rgb(0,0,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-shading: rgb(255,255,255)"><font face="宋体">经验值系统</font></span></b><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt 7.5pt 0pt 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; BACKGROUND: rgb(255,255,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-shading: rgb(255,255,255)"><font face="宋体">经验值（</font><font face="宋体">XP）是在经历过各种事情之后人物获得的某种提升，拥有常人所没有的非凡经历的人物自然也拥有比一般人更多的历练、经验和知识。经验值可以提升人物的各种技能，获得更多的技巧（专长），刻苦的锻炼还可以增强人物身体与心灵的强度。此外，轮回之境所给予的某些强化也需要额外的经验去理解、练习才能真正掌握。 
</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt 7.5pt 0pt 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; BACKGROUND: rgb(255,255,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-shading: rgb(255,255,255)"><font face="宋体">===========================================================================================================</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt 7.5pt 0pt 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; BACKGROUND: rgb(255,255,255); COLOR: rgb(255,0,0); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-shading: rgb(255,255,255)"><font face="宋体">经验值的获得：</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt 7.5pt 0pt 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; BACKGROUND: rgb(255,255,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-shading: rgb(255,255,255)"><font face="宋体">在无限恐怖</font><font face="宋体">TRPG中，每个玩家所获得的经验值根据其表现的优劣而定。一般来说，ST应该在每次跑团结束后，根据各人该次的表现记下其获得的经验；在每次冒险结束后，则再根据各人在该冒险世界中整体的表现给予其经验。在大多数情况下，ST可以在一次冒险世界结束的时候统一将这些经验发放；而若是玩家的人物在冒险世界（或冒险之外的某个章节）当中获得了较长的时间以休息、锻炼，ST也可以将其已获得的经验先行发放，以使其增加自己所需要锻炼的技能。 
</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt 7.5pt 0pt 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; BACKGROUND: rgb(255,255,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-shading: rgb(255,255,255)"><font face="宋体">一般来说，经验通过以下方式发放：</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt 7.5pt 0pt 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; BACKGROUND: rgb(255,255,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-shading: rgb(255,255,255)"><font face="宋体">将经验分为</font><font face="宋体">5个部分，根据PC的表现将5个部分相加，总计为PC经验。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt 7.5pt 0pt 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; BACKGROUND: rgb(255,255,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-shading: rgb(255,255,255)"><font face="宋体">第一部分：基础值，基础值为当前冒险世界的场次来决定，其在返回轮回之境时发放，所有角色获得</font><font face="宋体">2+2n点经验值，n为冒险世界的次数。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt 7.5pt 0pt 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; BACKGROUND: rgb(255,255,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-shading: rgb(255,255,255)"><font face="宋体">第一场冒险世界：</font><font face="宋体">4点XP，第二场冒险世界：6点XP，以此类推。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt 7.5pt 0pt 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; BACKGROUND: rgb(255,255,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-shading: rgb(255,255,255)"><font face="宋体">第二部分：奖励经验，奖励经验是计算本场所有角色获得的所有分数和支线的总计（计算固定奖励），角色们每获得了一个</font><font face="宋体">D，则获得3点经验，每有500分则获得2点经验，然后根据参团PC人数除以这个数，向上取整，每个PC获得对应的奖励经验，其在返回轮回之境时发放。</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt 7.5pt 0pt 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; BACKGROUND: rgb(255,255,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-shading: rgb(255,255,255)"><font face="宋体">第三部分：特殊表现，该经验的奖励内容如下，其在返回轮回之境时发放：</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; MARGIN: 0pt 7.5pt 0pt 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-pagination: widow-orphan"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; BACKGROUND: rgb(255,255,255); LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-shading: rgb(255,255,255)"><font face="宋体">1. 参团率：该PC完整的参与了一场跑团，期间没有挂机托管等行为，并且进行了基础的行动，给与2点经验。（该经验在当次跑团结束后立即发放）</font></span><span style="FONT-SIZE: 10.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; mso-spacerun: 'yes'; mso-font-kerning: 1.0000pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
